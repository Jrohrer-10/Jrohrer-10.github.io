About

Based in Greenville, SC, Rohrer Landscaping exists to give your property the attention it deserves.  From landscaping remodels, flowerbed restoration, hedge trimming/pruning, edging, mowing, to tree work, and more! We desire give the highest quality work at the most affordable price!

Whether you are a corporate company, a rental property owner, or just a regular homeowner looking to save some time, we are here for YOU. Give us a call today to get a free estimate!
